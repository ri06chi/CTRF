About Us

Chimera Translational Research Fraternity Pvt. Ltd. a company registered under Indian Companies Act, 2013 (CIN – U74999dl2018ptc328388).

The Chimera fraternity has extensive on ground experience in high end diagnostics and using this intellectual capital has developed and validated diagnostic kits indigenously. This translational research in the field of diagnostics would make transplant related investigations affordable and accessible to larger populations.

In tandem with vision of “Skill India” of honorable Prime Minister, we aim to educate and train the young generation of India about the latest research technology, skills and its implementation to generate interest in research to improve the human health and disease management.

Aim

Chimera Translational Research Fraternity (CTRF) private limited is established in year 2018 with the aim to carry on research/ innovation in bio-technology for translational research, diagnostics and bio therapeutics.

Vision

To set up individually or in association with leading professionals or professional bodies teaching and research training programs to enhance the cause of science towards betterment of mankind.

Mission

Pursue science with wisdom that enables research and innovation for the Indian scientific community and create a congenial environment to make India the global destination for translational research.
